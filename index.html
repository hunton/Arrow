--- layout: default title: Arrow - A DataSift Analytical Dashboard ---

Arrow is an analytical dashboard which will visulise a DataSift Stream or Export, simple! We've built it entirely on JavaScript and are open sourcing it for our users to fork, modify and play around with Arrow. We welcome feedback and bugs through our Issues page, and would love to know what you are developing with it. You can give Arrow a try right now, all you need is your API key, username and a Stream hash from DataSift.
